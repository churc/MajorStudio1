Images are adapted from installation shots from MoMA and from MoMA PS1 exhibition of L. Favaretto, photo by M. Septimus.
Paintings used from The Met collection by Florine Stettheimer and by Horace Pippin.
The numbers refer to The Met's Modern & Contemporary Art Collection - the artists who made the 14,350 artworks were identified by gender: 2,027 artworks are by women; 10,829 are by men;
48 are named artists but have not yet been identified by gender; 128 are couple / collaboratives with both women and men; and 1,318 artworks are by unknown artists. The Met csv data

To see quantitative visualizations about gender and artworks in The Met's Modern & Contemporary Art Collection, see Gender at The Met
