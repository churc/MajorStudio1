Modern & Contemporary Art Collection makers of the 14,350 artworks have been identified by gender: 2,027 artworks are by women and 10,829 are by men. These 12,856 artworks are shown in the charts.
The remaining 1,494 are ‘unidentified by maker’, or the artwork is by a named artist whose gender is not yet identified, or by an artist couple / collaborative with both women and men.
