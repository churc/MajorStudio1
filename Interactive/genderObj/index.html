Gender & Medium

A data visualization project by Clare Churchouse that investigates who are the most collected female and male artists in
The Metropolitan Museum of Art’s Modern & Contemporary Art Collection  -  and looks at connections between particular
mediums and gender

Makers of the Modern & Contemporary Art Collection's 14,350 artworks (as of January 2018) were identified by gender.
2,027 out of the total 14,350 artworks are by women and 10,829 are by men. These 12,856 artworks are shown in the charts. The remaining 1,494 are ‘unknown’ maker, or the artwork is by a named artist whose gender is not yet identified, or by an artist couple/collaborative with both women and men.
