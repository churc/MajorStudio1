For this project, we worked with The Metropolitan Museum of Art's publicly available database on github Background included an overview of The Met's holdings, departments and scope of materials, and the tracking of these holdings.
We looked at the data using csvkit. Gender is not tracked in any formal sense by all departments, and I was intrigued to gain a sense of something that could be ascertained but was not easily available, and look at this in relation to artwork medium.
A second project worked with publicly available images to select those that are described as 'uncertain.'
