Modern & Contemporary Art Collection makers of the 14,350 artworks have been identified by gender: 2,027 artworks are by women and 10,829 are by men. The remaining 1,494 are ‘unknown’, or the artwork is by a named artist whose gender is not yet identified, or is by an artist couple/collaborative with both women and men. This data is from The Met January 2018 Open Access csv. The Met's csv is updated weekly and is available on github, and The Met Collection API has now been launched.
