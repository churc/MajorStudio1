Modern & Contemporary Art Collection makers of the 14,350 artworks have been identified by gender: 2,027 artworks are by women and 10,829 are by men. The remaining 1,494 are ‘unidentified by maker’, or the artwork is by a named artist whose gender is not yet identified, or by an artist couple / collaborative with both women and men. This data is from The Met January 2018 csv.
The Met data open access csv is updated weekly and is available on github
